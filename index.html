I study problems in the intersection of machine learning and distributed systems. More recently, I've worked on libraries for reproducing results in distributed deep reinforcement learning.

I'm currently a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at UC Berkeley, advised by Joseph Gonzalez, Ion Stoica, and Ken Goldberg. I'm a member of the RISELab, the AUTOLAB, and the Berkeley AI Research Lab.

I currently work heavily on Ray, a distributed execution engine (mainly for Python).
